What is Home Basɘ?

Home Basɘ Collective, or lovingly, home Basɘ is a collective of Bass producers and DJs based out of Washington, DC. Founded by Uptown Vybz in 2022, it was created as a space (or a home Basɘ, if you will) for producers and fans of Bass music, especially Bass House, to come together and celebrate the music they love. Now they're banging Bass all through Chinatown, it's a Vybb.
